My Cute Cat

Luna is a fluffy white cat who loves to nap in the window and chase laser dots.

Her Favorite Things:

  • Sleeping on soft blankets

Luna’s favorite spot in the house is on a soft, fluffy blanket by the window. She curls up like a cinnamon roll and snoozes for hours!

Sometimes she kneads the blanket like it's dough before she settles in — it's her bedtime routine.

When the blanket is warm from the sun, Luna gets extra cozy. She even growls a little if anyone tries to move her. Yes, it's *her* blanket now!

  • Chasing Birds

Luna loves chasing birds! Every morning, she sits by the window, eyes wide and tail flicking.When she sees a bird outside, she makes funny little chirping sounds and jumps at the glass!

Her favorite targets are the quick little sparrows. Don’t worry — she never catches them, but she loves the game!
